Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026

Jubyr LLC (“Jubyr,” “we,” “us”) is a Philadelphia-based experience design studio. This policy covers jubyr.com, jubyr.tools, and the booking and event tools we run for our venues and clients, including Margolis. It explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

What we collect

When you book an event, reserve a table or couch, submit a DJ or performer inquiry, sign up for updates, or create a host/DJ account, we collect what you give us directly:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Event details you submit: date, guest count, performers, cover charge, Instagram handles, and similar booking information
  • Account details for host/DJ self-service accounts, including payout and W9 information where applicable
  • Anything you send us directly by email or through a contact form

We also use Mailchimp’s connected-site script on some pages to support our email list and forms. It may collect basic browsing information (like page views) the way most email and form tools do. We don’t run separate advertising trackers or sell any data collected this way.

How we use it

  • Process, confirm, and manage bookings and reservations
  • Send booking-related communications by email, phone, and text, including confirmations, calendar reminders, flyer and RSVP deadline nudges, and day-of coordination
  • Run host/DJ self-service accounts: lineup, flyer, and ticket-link submissions, profile completion reminders, and payouts
  • Send occasional updates about future Jubyr LLC events, only to people who’ve agreed to be contacted

Text messages (SMS)

We only text you if you’ve opted in, typically by checking a consent option on a booking form and providing your phone number. Message frequency varies: you’ll typically get a handful of texts tied to a specific booking or event (reminders, flyer and RSVP nudges) rather than a recurring schedule. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out of texts at any time by replying STOP to any message we send, and you can opt back in by contacting us directly. We don’t sell or share your phone number with third parties for their own marketing.

How we share information

We never sell your information. We share it only with the service providers we need to run our booking and communication tools (for example, email delivery, SMS delivery, and calendar scheduling), and only to the extent needed for them to provide that service to us, or when required by law.

Data storage

Information is stored on our own servers and kept only as long as it’s useful for the booking, account, or business record it belongs to.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the information we hold about you, unsubscribe from email updates using the link in any email, or opt out of texts by replying STOP. Email ruby@jubyr.com for any of this.

Children

Our sites and tools are not directed at children, and we don’t knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our tools and services change. The “last updated” date at the top will always reflect the latest version.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at ruby@jubyr.com.
