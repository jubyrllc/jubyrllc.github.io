02 · Our philosophy

You get paid. That’s the point.

Every event host is compensated through a partnership model we believe is one of the strongest and most community minded in the city. You are the heartbeat of the room, and we never want artists, DJs, or organizers to feel like they are working for free or being undervalued.

Free events

20% of bar sales

Proportionate compensation for bringing business into the venue and helping create a successful night.

Ticketed events

100% of door + 10% of bar

Every dollar collected at the door is yours, plus a cut of the bar you helped fill.

Talent perk

50% off drinks

DJs and performers get half off personal beverage purchases during their event.

We view this as a true partnership model — when you help us grow, we help you grow. We are not simply throwing parties. We are intentionally building community.

We prefer to work with DJs, hosts, promoters, and collectives who genuinely want to gather people, create experiences, and build moments that feel memorable, collaborative, and intentional. Our strongest nights are often intimate, joyful, community driven, and rooted in genuine connection.

So advertise thoughtfully. Rally your people. Build something meaningful. We encourage hosts to go beyond standard social media posting to make their event as intentional, impactful, and successful as possible. When you bring love into the room, we will take care of both you and your crowd with the same level of care and hospitality.

Community is everything to us. Let’s build something real together.

03 · Bar minimums

The numbers that keep the lights on.

Minimums exist so bartenders, barbacks, security, hosts, and support staff are compensated fairly, and the venue is staffed properly for your night. They are calculated on gross bar sales generated naturally during official event hours, and they are strictly enforced.

Margolis (inside)

Kitchen-closed hours$1,500 minimum
Kitchen-open hours$2,000 minimum
Friday + Saturday, 9 PM–2 AM$4,000 minimum

If the event does not organically meet the applicable minimum through guest spending during the event timeframe, the event organizer covers the remaining shortage and forfeits payout.

The Patio (events end by 10 PM, per city regulations)

Monday–Wednesday$1,000 · 1 bartender
Monday–Wednesday$1,500 · 2 bartenders
Thursday$1,500 · 2 bartenders
Sunday$1,500 · 2 bartenders
Friday$2,000 · 2 bartenders
Saturday$2,500 · 2 bartenders

Kitchen service: included during standard operating hours Friday and Saturday; available Monday–Thursday and Sunday with a $2,000 total minimum spend. Outside catering is permitted for all patio rentals. Additional servers available with a $200 guarantee per server.
