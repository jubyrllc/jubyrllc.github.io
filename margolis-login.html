The presale, in full

Why we sell entry before your promo is ready.

Guests who love Margolis commit early — often before a night’s lineup, flyer, or ticket link exists. Without a presale, those guests drift to other plans and the night loses them for good. The $20 presale simply holds them for your event while your promo comes together, so neither you nor the venue loses potential guests while we wait.

It never touches your money. You keep 100% of your ticket sales and door sales, always. The presale ends the moment your lineup, flyer, and ticket link are approved — from then on every guest is sent to your link. Presale guests are venue-loyal turnout added on top of your crowd, not taken out of it.

Need a night without presale? For parties catered to strict demographics — LGBTQ-only nights, guaranteed sell-outs (with proof), private events, and similar — DJs and hosts may request that no presale run for their night. Margolis reserves the right to deny the request within reason. Ask by replying to your confirmation email or writing ruby@margolisphilly.com.
