Margolis guests · Advance reservations

Lock your night before tickets drop.

Pick an upcoming event and reserve guaranteed entry for $20 a person — even before the host's ticket link is live. Bringing friends? 2 tickets for $30, 4 for $50.

How it works: an advance reservation guarantees entry to the event you pick — $20 per person, and every ticket after the first is just $10 (2 tickets $30, 3 tickets $40, 4 tickets $50). Once the host's ticket link goes live, we point you there instead — and honestly, waiting for tickets will usually be cheaper. This is for the nights you refuse to miss.

The fine print: reservations are non-refundable. Can't make it anymore? Reach out at least 48 hours before the event and we'll transfer your tickets to another night — within 48 hours of the event there are no transfers and no refunds. The one exception: if a host takes an event invite-only and we can't honor your reservation, you're refunded in full. Entry is only during posted event hours; we're not open outside of events. Your reservation isn't confirmed until it's paid.
